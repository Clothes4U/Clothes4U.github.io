HeartBridge 心桥

By Clothes

Published 08/08/2666

Introduction 简介

HeartBridge is the magazine of School of Mathematical Sciences(SMS), Peking University. It covers articles and photos about students’ activities and thoughts in SMS. The first issue was published in 1996 and there are 57 issues until 2022 (including 2 special issues for the 20th anniversary). I(Fan Ye) was the chief editor from Sep. 2016 to Jan. 2018.

《心桥》是北京大学数学科学学院的院刊，用以展现数院学生的生活见闻和所思所想。第一期在1996年发行，到2020年一共有55期（包括两期二十周年特别刊）。我（叶帆）曾于2016年9月至2018年1月担任主编。
