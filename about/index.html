About Me

By Clothes

Published 03/28/2021

Fan Ye 叶 帆

Email: fy260 at cam.ac.uk

Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Maths at University of Cambridge. My supervisor is Jacob Rasmussen. You can see my complete CV here.

I am interested in low-dimensional topology, especially about gauge theory (Instanton, monopole, Heegaard Floer homology) and knot polynomials (Khovanov homology, $\mathfrak{sl}_n$ homology, HOMFLY-PT polynomial). I also have interests in symplectic geometry and mathematical physics.

Research

  • Instanton Floer homology, sutures, and Euler characteristics
    (With Zhenkun Li, submitted) ArXiv:2101.05169 PDF
    Abstract: This is a companion paper to an earlier work of the authors. This paper introduces an axiomatic way to define sutured homology for balanced sutured manifolds and calculate its graded Euler characteristic. It follows that graded Euler characteristics of sutured instanton homology $SHI$ and sutured (Heegaard) Floer homology $SFH$ are equal under a mild homology condition. This paper also discusses the Euler characteristic of framed instanton homology $I^\sharp$ with respect to torsion spin$^c$ structures defined in the earlier work. Further results about removing the homology condition are in progress.

  • Sutured instanton homology and Heegaard diagrams
    (With John A. Baldwin and Zhenkun Li, submitted) ArXiv:2011.09424 PDF
    Abstract: This paper provides an upper bound on the dimension of sutured instanton homology $SHI$ from the sutured Heegaard Floer chain complex. This gives an alternative proof of the result about $(1,1)$ knots in the previous paper. Moreover, it follows that strong L-spaces are instanton L-spaces. Further results about the relation between instanton theory and Heegaard Floer theory are in progress.

  • Instanton Floer homology, sutures, and Heegaard diagrams
    (With Zhenkun Li, submitted) ArXiv:2010.07836 PDF
    Abstract: This paper has two parts. The first part introduces a method to calculate sutured instanton homology $SHI$ from Heegaard diagrams. An upper bound on the dimension of instanton knot homology $KHI$ is obtained for $(1,1)$ knots. The second part introduces a decomposition of framed instanton homology $I^\sharp$ related to torsion spin$^c$ structures. Many other properties of instanton theory are obtained.

  • Constrained knots in lens spaces
    (Submitted) ArXiv:2007.04237 PDF List of constrained knots
    Abstract: This paper introduces a family of $(1,1)$ knots called constrained knots, which generalizes 2-bridge knots. A complete classification and many properties of constrained knots are obtained. The relation between constrained knots and hyperbolic manifolds can be found in the above list.

  • Trisections of four manifolds
    Senior thesis, supervised by Clifford Taubes and Yi Liu
    Abstract: This is a review paper about trisections, which is a generalization of Heegaard splittings in dimension 4.
